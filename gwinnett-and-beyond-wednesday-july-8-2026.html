MARKET SNAPSHOT
Preparation matters more than ever

When buyers have choices, they tend to slow down and compare homes more carefully.

That means the small details matter: clean rooms, completed repairs, good lighting, a comfortable temperature, and a price that makes sense beside nearby competition.

The simple version: sellers do not need a perfect home, but they do need a thoughtful plan. Good preparation can help a home feel like the easy choice.
HOMEOWNER TIP OF THE WEEK
Give your air conditioner a five-minute check.

Georgia heat makes the air conditioner work hard. This week, take a quick look at a few basics:

  • Check whether the air filter needs replacing.
  • Make sure furniture or curtains are not blocking indoor vents.
  • Look around the outdoor unit and clear away leaves or plants.
  • Watch for unusual sounds, weak airflow, or water where it should not be.

Catching a small issue early is usually easier than discovering it on the hottest afternoon of the year. If something looks wrong, call a qualified HVAC professional.
AROUND GWINNETT: LOCAL EVENT OF THE WEEK

If you are looking for something local this weekend, here is one Gwinnett event worth checking out.

Suwanee Celebrates America 250

The City of Suwanee is hosting a patriotic community celebration on Saturday, July 11, from 3:00 PM to 9:00 PM at Town Center Park. The afternoon and evening include live music, family activities, friendly contests, food and artisan vendors, and a Liberty Lap for children with bikes, wagons, scooters, or strollers.

Tip: Bring lawn chairs, arrive early for activities, and check the official event page for weather or parking updates.
