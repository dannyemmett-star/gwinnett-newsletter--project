7

What happens after everything is complete

Once the brief and brand files are delivered, the first issue moves through four clear stages.

1. Brand build

Danny creates a private customer configuration using her approved name, colors, links, market, and content choices.

2. First proof

She receives a browser preview and checks the design, wording, contact details, disclosures, and links.

3. One revision

She returns one organized list of corrections. Danny updates the newsletter and produces a final test version.

4. Test and launch

The issue is sent to a test recipient, checked on desktop and mobile, then placed into her email platform for sending.
